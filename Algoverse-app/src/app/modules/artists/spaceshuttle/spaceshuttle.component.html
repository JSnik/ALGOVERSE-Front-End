Spaceshuttle

The spaceshuttle will send one artist to the moon and beyond. The the community of VERSE holders decides once a month which artist is send to the shuttle. The winner will be displayed on the front page and created offers will be highlighted on the marketplace. The winner receives a badge for a won period. This badge is displayed next to their user name through out the marketplace for a life time.
